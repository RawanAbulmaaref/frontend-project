Senior Full Stack Engineer
(React/Node.js)

Innovate Solutions Inc.

San Francisco, CA (Remote
Friendly)

Full Time,
Remote

$120,000 - $150,000 / year

Innovate Solutions is seeking a highly skilled and motivated Senior Full Stack Engineer to join our dynamic team. You will be instrumental in designing, developing, and maintaining scalable web applications using modern technologies. This role offers the opportunity to work on exciting new features, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and contribute significantly to our product roadmap. We value innovation, clean code, and a passion for solving complex problems.

Responsibilities

Design, develop, and deploy robust web applications using React.js and Node.js.

Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.

Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers to define and implement new features.

Participate in code reviews to maintain high code quality and share knowledge.

Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize application performance.

Contribute to architectural discussions and decisions.

Mentor junior developers and foster a culture of continuous learning.

Requirements

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.

5+ years of professional experience in full-stack web development.

Strong proficiency in React.js and its ecosystem (Redux, Hooks, etc.).

Extensive experience with Node.js and building RESTful APIs.

Familiarity with relational databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, MySQL) and NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB).

Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).

Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git.

Excellent problem-solving, communication, and interpersonal skills.

Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment.

Our mission is to deliver innovative, reliable, and user-centric software that drives success for our clients worldwide.
